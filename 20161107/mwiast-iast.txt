Upper-case IAST
All Sanskrit words in italics may start with a capital letter.
The capitalization is irrelevant as far as the correspondence
to the underlying Devanagari (or SLP1) spelling.
The capital letters which occur with diacritics in sanskrit italics are:
Ā, Ć, Ń, Ś, Ū, Ḍ, Ṇ, Ṛ, Ṭ
